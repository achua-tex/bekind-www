Features

Kindness Tracking

Did It, Will Do, Skip, Hide - Make the world a better place, one act, one day, at a time. Custom Idea - Act on our suggested ideas, or do your own. Kind Act History - Keep track of your good deeds, customizable with your own stories and pictures. Gamification (Coins, Streaks, Levels/Badges) - Earn coins, level up, and have fun.

Kindness Sharing

Kind Act Sharing - Share ideas and stories with others, and inspire goodwill. Sharing - Share inspiring stories with your friends and family. Mood - Celebrate during the good times. Comfort during the bad ones. Notifications - Stay informed of new ideas, the latest news, new friends, new followers and new comments.

Community Building

Social Network for Kindness - Connect with BeKind users all over the world. Foster a community of kind and loving people. Friends - Get inspired by friends. Peer to Peer Chat - Communicate with other users. Leaderboard - Get inspired by top users from around the world.

Safety Features

User Blocking - Block trolls and spammers. Content Blocking - Keeps conversations positive by blocking obscene language. Contact Us - Suggest ideas, send feedback, contact support, or volunteer, all within the app.

Mobile Features

iOS and Android. Authentication - Login using Email, Facebook, or Google. Offline Support - Many features work even without an internet connection.
